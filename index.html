Rohan Chitnis

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley in the department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences.

I conduct research primarily in robotics, with Professor Pieter Abbeel's Robot Learning Lab. My main interests are in hierarchical planning, which seeks to get robots to execute tasks that require a long sequence of steps, like setting a dinner table. I have recently been exploring reinforcement learning techniques for improving the performance of hierarchical planning systems. Check out my publications for more information.

I also dabble in NLP (Natural Language Processing), and I've done some work in neural machine translation with John Denero as part of the Oscii Lab.

I enjoy being a TA for classes. Helping people feels good.

My primary hobby and source of stress relief is running. A really cool person I recently met also helped me get into reading. Here are some good books.
