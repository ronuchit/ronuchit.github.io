Dynamic Viewing
Our final project for CS280, Computer Vision. We developed an algorithm to quickly track a user's face position, rotation, and distance using pyramid SSD template matching. Based on the tracking, we dynamically alter the center of projection of the monitor screen to offer a more realistic viewing experience.
